Where to find us!

    A Shot of Java

    7003 N 58th Ave Glendale, AZ 85301 map
    A real Glendale hole-in-the-wall gem (it's even difficult to find!). She brews all her tea and drip coffee on the spot though making each cup taste amazing.

    Coronado Cafe

    2201 N 7th St, Phoenix, AZ map
    An elegant restaurant located in a restored historic house. Everything about this place is great; the atmosphere, food, staff, prices and the drinks. Everything.

    Fresh Vitamins

    14700 N Frank Lloyd Wright Blvd Scottsdale, AZ 85260 map
    There's a neat little coffee bar hidden in the back of this Fresh Vitamins.. they also serve amazing fruit smoothies.

    Lydia runs this coffee cart out of the Swap Mart and does catering. Email Lydia at johnniesjava@hotmail.com if you're interested in her catering services.
