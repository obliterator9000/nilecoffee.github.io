About Us!

We are the original! Lo Fi Coffee, Mesa was the first location founded in 2011 in partnership with The Nile Theater. Our goal is to provide first and foremost a great location for artists and musicians. We cater to concerts being held in The Nile Theater.

Take a peak at our event calendar! We are always having a lot of local concerts. Almost all of our concerts will have a mix of both touring band and local musicians on it. We strive to create an environment that is friendly to everyone!

Booking with us is easy! We often will wait for the right opportunities to hold our concerts, so shoot us an email at loficoffee@gmail.com. We'll get back to you as fast possible!

Drinks

All of our drinks are created with our locally roasted espresso we roast at the Lo Fi Coffee Roastery. We serve our hot drinks in 8oz, 12oz, and 16oz; we also serve iced drinks in 16oz and 20oz sizes. Ask about our mixtures and our ratios! We're always more than happy to explain the science between our drink making. Keep an eye on this section and we will create a compendium of drinks and how we make them!

  • Latte - Espresso, Milk, and foam(with 15 flavors to choose from)
  • Mocha - Espresso, Milk, Foam, and Chocolate Flavoring
  • Cappucino - Classic: 2 parts espresso, 2 parts steamed milk, 2 parts foam

Baked Goods

All of our baked goods are baked in house(except our bagels). We cater to both vegan and non-vegans with our baked goods. We normally bake in the mornings, so make sure to come in and get them fresh!

Bagel Sandwiches

All of our bagel sandwiches come on a bagel with two locally sourced eggs, and choice ingredients. Cost: 4.50

  • The New York(or 'The Chad'): The original!!! This great sandwich comes with thick cut bacon, garden grown basil, and a slice of swiss cheese with a thick slice of tomato.
  • The Greek: A Delicious morning pickmeup, or a easy quick lunch. Feta cheese heated with the egg, baby spinach, fresh basil pesto, and a slice of tomato.
  • The Cowboy: Hands down the most popular! A generous portion of Chorizo, fresh cut guacamole, and smothered in green chiles!
